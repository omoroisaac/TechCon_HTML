Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in San Francisco. What started as a modest event has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from across the globe.

Over the years, TechCon has expanded to include satellite events in Europe and Asia, while maintaining its core mission of fostering innovation and collaboration in the tech community. Our 2024 edition marks our 15th anniversary, and we're planning our most ambitious program yet.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. Our mission is threefold:

  • To educate and inspire the next generation of technology leaders
  • To create a platform for meaningful dialogue about technology's role in society
  • To foster connections that lead to real innovation

We believe technology should be accessible, ethical, and beneficial to all, and this principle guides every aspect of our conference programming.
